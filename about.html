About the project

What is ErgoLite?

A free, practical resource to help remote workers build healthier, more comfortable workspaces.

Our mission

ErgoLite was built with one simple goal: make ergonomics advice accessible to everyone. Most guides are either too technical or locked behind paywalls. We think good posture information should be free.

Whether you're working from a home office, a co-working space, or a kitchen table — the ErgoLite guide gives you the essentials to set up a healthier desk without spending money on equipment.

💡 ErgoLite is a practice project built to explore Google Ads optimization for digital products. All resources are genuinely free.

Who is this for?

Remote workers, freelancers, students, and anyone who spends several hours a day at a desk. You don't need fancy equipment or a standing desk. The guide works with whatever setup you already have.

What's inside the guide?

The ErgoLite guide covers chair height and lumbar positioning, monitor placement and eye distance, keyboard and mouse ergonomics, lighting tips to reduce eye strain, and a 10-minute daily reset routine — all in plain language, no jargon.
