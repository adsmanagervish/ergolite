Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Effective: January 1, 2025

ErgoLite ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

ErgoLite does not require account creation or email signup. We do not collect personally identifiable information from visitors unless you choose to contact us directly.

When you contact us, we may collect:

  • Your name (optional)
  • Your email address
  • The message you send

Cookies & Analytics

This website may use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. Google Analytics collects anonymous usage data such as pages visited, time on site, and general location (country/region). No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

If you are visiting via a Google Ad, Google may use cookies to measure ad performance. You can opt out of Google's use of cookies by visiting Google Ads Settings.

How We Use Information

Any information you provide via our contact form is used only to respond to your inquiry. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies separately.

Data Retention

Contact form messages are retained only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry. We do not store personal data indefinitely.

Your Rights

You have the right to request deletion of any personal data we may hold. To make such a request, please contact us using the details on our Contact page.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please reach us via our Contact page.
